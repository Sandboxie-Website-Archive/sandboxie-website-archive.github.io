Re: SbieCtrl.exe unable to start correctly (0xc000007b)

Something may have mixed up 32/64 versions of the CRT dlls. Uninstall Sbie and reinstall. If you still have the problem, delete these files by hand and then reinstall Sbie.

\windows\system32\mfc100u.dll
\windows\system32\msvcr100.dll
\windows\sysWOW64\mfc100u.dll
\windows\sysWOW64\msvcr100.dll
